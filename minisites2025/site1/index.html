Presentation

🎯 Aim of the Game

  • Become the smartest player through “buying” scientist.
  • The game ends when someone finishes their final year (Terminale) and gets their bac.

🎲 Equipment

  • The game board
  • 2 dice
  • ... scientists' cards
  • ... chance cards
  • ... knowledge points

🎮 Gameplay

  • Players take turns in order:
    • Roll both dice and move that number of spaces clockwise.
    • Depending on the space:
      • Unowned scientist: buy him
      • Owned property: Pay research fees to the owner.
      • Chance / Community Chest: Draw top card and follow instructions.
      • Go to exam room: Pass the exam or pay with your knowledge points if you fail.
  • When passing GO, collect ...

📚 Buying a Scientist

  • Land on an unowned scientist: You can buy it for the price on the title deed.
  • If you decline to buy: The teacher auctions it to the highest bidder.
  • When you have a full set of scientists, you get their lab.

💰 Research Fees

  • Pay fees when landing on another player’s scientist.
  • Research fee amounts are shown on their card.
  • Fees increase with lab upgrades.

📝 Exam Room

  • You go to the exam room if:
    • You land on the "exam room" space.
    • You draw a "Go to exam room" card.
    • You roll 3 doubles in a row.
  • To get out:
    • Pass the exam.
    • Use a "Get out of exam room free" card.
    • Roll doubles on one of your next 3 turns.
    • If you didn't get out in 3 turns, pay knowledge points and move.

Aim of the game

  • Become the smartest player through “buying” scientist.
  • The game ends when someone finished his final year (Terminale) and got his bac.

Currency

  • Knowledge point
  • Money

Trading

  • Players may trade:
    • Money
    • Knowledge points
    • Scientist
    • "Get out of exam room free" cards
  • Players cannot trade:
    • Buildings (must be sold first)
    • Future promises (like immunity from rent)

Winning the game

The game ends when a terminal ended his year and got his bac. They must collect enough knowledge points and scientists to be the smartest in the room. To finish your year you got to do in total 7 rows one for each semesters. Indeed 3 in seconde, 2 in premiere and finally also 2 in terminale. But you still got to be acknowledged enough to handle the studies. The scientist you’ll buy will help. The winner will be the first to finish if he has enough knowledge points.
